Why Donate?

The unjust deaths of George Floyd, Ahmaud Arbery, Breanna Taylor & many more black people have caused nationwide protests to quell the injustice. Thousands of protesters, who have not been proven guilty, are in jail. Your contribution will provide bail for those who cannot afford it.

Listen to George Floyd: "I can't breathe," as the Cop, Derek Chauvin, holds his neck to the ground.
